From 'Hello' to a Real Conversation

Follow a natural path of connection. Each step brings you closer.

1. Share Your World

Start with a simple text. Break the ice by sending a photo of your day—your morning coffee, a book you're reading, or a view from your window. See how they react.

2. Let Them Hear You

Tired of typing? Record and send a quick voice memo. Let them hear the nuance in your voice, practice your pronunciation out loud, and get a more personal, detailed response to your spoken thoughts.

3. Make the Call

When you're ready, jump into a seamless, real-time voice call. No scheduling, no pressure. Just a natural conversation, whenever you feel like it.

4. Relive the Moment

After your call, get a personalized summary. Revisit key topics, check new vocabulary, and see how your connection is growing over time.
